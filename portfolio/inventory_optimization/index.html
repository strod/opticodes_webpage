Data-Driven Component Matching

Traditionally, managing inventory and sourcing components for production involved manual effort, guesswork, and often resulted in excess inventory or costly purchase orders. Opticode Solutions leverages a robust material description database to empower your business to:

  • Reduce Excess Inventory: Excess inventory ties up valuable capital and resources. Our application helps you identify similar components already in stock, reducing the need for overstocking and saving you money.
  • Streamline Component Sourcing: Searching for the right components can be a time-consuming process. Our app simplifies this by providing a similarity score, making it easier to find suitable alternatives quickly.
  • Minimize Purchase Orders: By offering alternatives that you already have in your inventory, we minimize the need for additional purchase orders, resulting in cost savings and improved procurement efficiency.
  • Enhance Manufacturing Efficiency: Reduced inventory and more streamlined component sourcing lead to increased manufacturing efficiency, helping you meet production deadlines and customer demands.
